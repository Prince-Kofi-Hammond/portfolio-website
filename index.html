👋 I'm Prince Hammond

A Computer Science student who turns caffeine into code and ideas into interactive experiences.

My journey into development started not in a classroom, but with the pure curiosity of wanting to build something that works. The first time I made a button change color with JavaScript, I was hooked. Whether it’s crafting responsive frontends with React or designing efficient backends with Node.js, I love the process of solving real problems with code. Currently, I’m diving deeper into full‑stack development and exploring how technology can be both powerful and human‑centered.

What drives me:

  • 🤝 Learning through collaboration and open source
  • 💡 Turning “what if” into “what’s next”
